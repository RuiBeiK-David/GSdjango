About Us

The Health Monitoring System is a platform dedicated to providing real-time health monitoring solutions for families. Our mission is to help family members better monitor and manage the health of elderly people or patients through advanced technology.

Our team consists of professionals from Tianjin Polytechnic University, including software engineers, medical experts, and user experience designers. We are committed to providing the highest quality health monitoring services, ensuring that every family can easily access and manage health data.

Core Features

Real-time Monitoring

24/7 continuous monitoring of vital signs, including heart rate, blood pressure, and body temperature.

Smart Alerts

Automatic alerts for abnormal data, supporting multiple notification methods to ensure timely detection of health risks.

Data Analysis

Professional health data analysis, generating intuitive reports to help understand health trends.
